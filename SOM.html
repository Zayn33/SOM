About Us

Society of Motivation is a platform that will bring all the positive people together and give them a chance to spread their message through writing, speaking and social work in their surroundings and on social media platforms.

Our Beginning

It all starts with an idea that all the experienced and non-experienced Motivational writers, speakers and social workesrs should work together in this society for the noble cause of spreading motivation in the society. SOM will spread the message through online sessions and physical sessions on different Psychological,Philosophical and Social topics.

Our Mission

To make people realize that mental health is more important than materialistic things.
To motivate people to work for their goals and aims.

Our Vision

To highlight the importance of Writing and Speaking in our society
To work for Humanity through social work and motivation.

Our Plans

Our plan is not to make you survive in this competetive world, rather we want you to be the best version of your self by enhancing your verbal and comprehensive skills and to make you a better being, not just for yourself but also for the others.

Stories

Stories that inspires millions of people as per saying "Stories are a communal currency of humanity."

Writing Department

Writing is an important source of conveying any message in written form and in attractive words to catch anyone's attention. Writing Department will play the role of motivating people through Motivational articles, Stories, blogs, poetries and other types of Writings.

Speaking Department

Speaking is the biggest element for convincing people to your point. Speaking Department will motivate people through online and physical sessions, live sessions and discussions, hostings, speeches and debates.

Social Work Department

Social work is actually living and serving for humanity. Social work department will motivate people through social work like blood donation, clothes drives, food drives, fees cases and marriage cases etc.
